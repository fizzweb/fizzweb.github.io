Falafel burgers

Prep: 10 MINS Cook: 6 MINS Serves: 4


A healthy burger that's filling too. These are great for anyone who craves a big bite but doesn't want the calories.

Pureed chickpeas, seasoned with the characteristic flavors of falafel, make excellent veggie burgers.

Steps:

Step 1: Drain a 400g can chickpeas and pat dry with kitchen paper.

Step 2: Tip into a food processor along with 1 small roughly chopped red onion, 1 garlic clove, handful of flat-leaf parsley, 1 tsp ground cumin, 1 tsp ground coriander, ½ tsp harissa paste or chillli powder, 2 tbsp plain flour and a little salt.

Step 3: Blend until fairly smooth, then shape into four patties with your hands.

Step 1: Heat 2 tbsp sunflower oil in a non-stick frying pan, add the burgers, then quickly fry for 3 mins on each side until lightly golden.

Step 1: Serve with toasted pitta bread, 200g tub tomato salsa and a green salad.

Ingredients


  • 400g can chickpea, rinsed and drained

  • 1 small red onion, roughly chopped

  • 1 garlic clove, chopped

  • handful of flat-leaf parsley or curly parsley

  • 1 tsp ground cumin

  • 1 tsp ground coriander

  • 1⁄2 tsp harissa paste or chilli powder

  • 2 tbsp plain flour

  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil

  • toasted pitta bread, to serve

  • 200g tub tomato salsa, to serve

  • green salad, to serve
